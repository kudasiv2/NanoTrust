FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is CenturyWealth and how does it work?
CenturyWealth is a decentralized wealth management platform powered by PancakeSwap V3 LP. Users deposit USDT which is then supplied to PancakeSwap V3 LP to generate yields. Investors earn 1.2% base daily ROI from the full deposit amount, with additional boosts based on their leadership rank (up to +1% for Diamond Rank = 2.2% daily). The platform operates fully on-chain with transparent smart contracts.
What is the minimum investment amount?
The minimum investment amount is 5 USDT. You can have up to 50 active deposits per wallet. A 10% management fee is deducted from each deposit, with the remaining 90% being used for yield generation. Daily ROI is calculated from your full deposit amount (before fee).
How does the 100-day lock period work?
All investments have a 100-day lock period starting from your deposit time. During this period, you can claim your daily ROI earnings without restrictions. However, if you choose to withdraw your capital before the 100 days complete, a 30% early withdrawal fee will apply. After 100 days, you can withdraw your full capital without any fees.
How do leadership ranks and ROI boosts work?
There are 5 leadership ranks (Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond) that provide ROI boosts from 0.1% to 1%. Ranks are based solely on Team Volume:

• Bronze: 5,000 USDT team volume
• Silver: 15,000 USDT team volume
• Gold: 30,000 USDT team volume
• Platinum: 50,000 USDT team volume
• Diamond: 100,000 USDT team volume

Rank boosts are permanent and apply to all your active deposits. Daily ROI is calculated from your full deposit amount (not net after fee).
What are the referral commission rates?
CenturyWealth offers a 5-level referral system with commissions:
• Level 1 (Direct): 5% (always active)
• Level 2: 3% (requires 3 qualified directs)
• Level 3: 2% (requires 5 qualified directs)
• Level 4: 1% (requires 8 qualified directs)
• Level 5: 0.5% (requires 10 qualified directs)

A "qualified direct" is a referral with at least 50 USDT total active deposit.
How many deposits can I have?
You can have up to 50 active deposits per wallet. Each deposit is tracked separately with its own lock period and ROI calculation. This allows you to manage your investments flexibly and stagger your deposits over time.
Is the smart contract secure?
Yes, CenturyWealth uses OpenZeppelin's battle-tested contracts including ReentrancyGuard and SafeERC20. The contract includes security features like:
• Reentrancy protection on all state-changing functions
• SafeERC20 for secure token transfers
• Only EOA (Externally Owned Account) restriction for investments

The contract is verified on BSCScan.

How is the daily ROI calculated?
Daily ROI is calculated per deposit as:

(Full Deposit Amount × (Base ROI + Rank Boost)) / 10000

Where:
• Base ROI = 120 (1.2%)
• Rank Boost = 10-100 (0.1% - 1%) depending on your rank
• Calculation is based on time elapsed since last claim
• ROI is calculated from your full deposit amount (not net after fee)

Example: With 1,000 USDT deposit and Gold rank (+0.3%):
Daily ROI = 1000 × (120 + 30) / 10000 = 15 USDT per day (1.5%)
