Frequently Asked Questions

What is NanoTether and how does it work?
NanoTether is a decentralized yield infrastructure powered by Venus Protocol. Users deposit USDT which is then supplied to Venus Protocol to generate yields. Investors earn 0.3% base daily ROI, with additional boosts based on their leadership rank (up to +1% for Diamond Rank = 1.3% daily). The platform operates fully on-chain with transparent smart contracts audited by OpenZeppelin standards.
What is the minimum investment amount?
The minimum investment amount is 10 USDT. There is no maximum limit. A 10% management fee is deducted from each deposit (sent to the management wallet), with the remaining 90% being deposited to Venus Protocol to start generating yields immediately.
How does the 60-day lock period work?
All investments have a 60-day lock period starting from your deposit time. During this period, you can claim your daily ROI earnings without restrictions. However, if you choose to withdraw your capital before the 60 days complete, a 50% early withdrawal fee will apply (calculated on your active deposit amount). After 60 days, you can withdraw your full capital without any fees.
